Another Guide to Centering a Div

Centering a div, with CSS, has never been as obvious as you would expect. Centering text inside a div is straight forward with the text-align property, but then it starts to get tricky with internal boxes. When you want to vertically center a div, CSS, and some of its proponents, appear to stand directly against you.

The aim of this page is to show how, by completely giving up on CSS for layouts, any div can be centered; horizontally and vertically. Just give up and use physics.
